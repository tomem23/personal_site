Renew

Designing a physical therapy app

Renew is a work in progress for a physical therapy mobile app that helps users plan their rehabilitation for certain injuries. The goal is to make it easy for people to schedule and track their exercises. Motivation from other users will also be a key aspect for the application.

User Research

I interviewed people who had currently suffered an injury that required going through physical therapy. I followed this by creating a persona for the app and a scenario in which it would be used.
