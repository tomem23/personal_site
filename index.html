Hi, I'm Tom Mudgett

I'm a Designer and a Strategist

I'm based in New York City but I'm originally from Tegucigalpa, Honduras. I like to build user-centered products and services. From designing logos to creating business models, I use my background in business and design to develop ideas and convert them into opportunities for everyone involved.

Skills

Design

I like to solve problems, which is what design is essentially about. It’s not only about creating things that are aesthetically pleasing, but creating things that work. My goal is to improve the world around us through smart and thoughtful design.

Strategy

Throughout my career I’ve been able to combine creative thinking with business strategy to approach different challenges. I’ve had formal training and experience in marketing, strategy, and operations, which allows me to understand complex business models and systems.

User Experience

This perspective is what allows me to define and frame projects. In every step, when I design or build something, I’m always trying to examine and consider what the user needs. My aim is always to make complex systems more user-friendly and clear.
